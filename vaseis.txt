Purchases : 

GET '/purchases' renders All purchases with some stats
GET '/purchases/:store_id' at store with id=?
GET '/purchases/:store_id?{query}' query filters

Optional:
query = "
	date='DD-MM-YYYY'&
	maxUnitsBought='.'&minUnitsBought='.'&
	maxTotal='.'&minTotal='.'&
	paymentMethod='cash|card'&
	category='.'
	"
